Sierra Leone - COVID19 App

CoronaInfo App is designed to track the status of COVID19 daily cases for Sierra Leone country. It keeps people updated with statistics of cases all over African continent and Globe, helps Sierra citizens to report covid cases nearby their locality and allows them to take a self test.
